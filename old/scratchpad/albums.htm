bhool ma
bhulyo

 


the cover had to be as good as the album, I tried it and someone (i think twaka) later said that  the cover turned out to be better than the album, :) but it was supposed to be something different in the begining, I went talk to Robin and with other band mates, so he came up with this idea that what if I could somehow get a 'peace' sign in the front... he wanted it in the memory of victims of maoist insurgency, 9/11 etc etc.

. ..then the work started,  we decided what if we use pictures.. lot of them ?  that same night I went to Min dai with maka and got hold of lot of pictures from him, then I needed a scanner, surendra fetched that from binod and then - scan and resize and design and redeseign and so on... finally this thing came out ! I try to put  some rationale behind every work of mine, this one has too, may be some of it is reflected by the songs too..


I might have worked harder than required as I don't know all the tricks of the tools I use, but this was quite a bit of playing around. especially the handwritten lyrics by paka and rizu had to be there above the images, making that happen was fun for me at least. The B/W white poster had two new 'improvised' logos but that poster never made it to the production.. sometimes simplicity wins..  overall I am happy with this, very happy :)
